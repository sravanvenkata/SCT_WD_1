Welcome to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the finest magical institution in the wizarding world. Discover the magic, adventure, and history that make Hogwarts truly special.

Houses

Gryffindor

Known for bravery, courage, and chivalry. Founded by Godric Gryffindor.

Hufflepuff

Values loyalty, patience, and fair play. Founded by Helga Hufflepuff.

Ravenclaw

Celebrates intelligence, wisdom, and creativity. Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw.

Slytherin

Prides itself on ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. Founded by Salazar Slytherin.

Professors

Albus Dumbledore

Headmaster of Hogwarts. Widely regarded as the greatest wizard of his time, Dumbledore was known for his immense power, wisdom, and unwavering belief in the good of others.

Minerva McGonagall

Head of Gryffindor and Transfiguration professor. A stern but deeply fair witch, she is a powerful Animagus and a key member of the Order of the Phoenix.

Severus Snape

Potions Master and Head of Slytherin House. A famously complex and enigmatic wizard whose true loyalties remained a secret to all but a select few.

Filius Flitwick

Charms Master and Head of Ravenclaw House. Despite his small stature, he is a formidable wizard and a former dueling champion with a cheerful disposition.

Pomona Sprout

Herbology Professor and Head of Hufflepuff House. A kind and nurturing witch with an extensive and unparalleled knowledge of magical plants and fungi.

Sybill Trelawney

Professor of Divination. The great-great-granddaughter of a celebrated Seer, she is known for making genuine, crucial prophecies at unpredictable moments.

Castle

The Hogwarts castle is a sprawling, enchanted fortress filled with secret passages, moving staircases, and magical classrooms. Its towers and dungeons hold centuries of magical history.

The Great Hall: The heart of Hogwarts, where students gather for meals, ceremonies, and celebrations. Its enchanted ceiling mirrors the sky outside, and it is the site of the Sorting Ceremony and many magical feasts.
The Library: A vast, quiet sanctuary filled with thousands of magical books and ancient tomes. The library is a favorite spot for Ravenclaws and anyone seeking knowledge, but beware the Restricted Section!
Potions Classroom: Deep in the dungeons, Professor Snape's potions class is both feared and respected. Here, students learn the subtle science and exact art of potion-making, under the watchful eye of the Potions Master.
